rCommon Privacy Policy

rCommon is built with a commitment to user privacy. As a shared local library, rCommon does not track, collect, or transmit your personal data.

Data Collection and Telemetry
rCommon does not include any analytics, crash reporting platforms, or tracking metrics. It does not collect or request any personal identifiers, email addresses, or system credentials.

Network Connections
rCommon runs completely offline and does not make any network connections on its own (except when applications explicitly invoke helper functions to fetch download feeds).

Local Data Storage
rCommon is a library and does not persist configurations or data on its own. Applications consuming rCommon manage their own files and storage.
