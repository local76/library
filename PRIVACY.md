rTemplate Privacy Policy

rTemplate is built with a commitment to user privacy. As a local terminal application template, rTemplate does not track, collect, or transmit your personal data.

Data Collection and Telemetry
rTemplate does not include any analytics, crash reporting platforms, or tracking metrics. It does not collect or request any personal identifiers, email addresses, or system credentials.

Network Connections
rTemplate runs completely offline and does not make any network connections.

Local Data Storage
All data managed by rTemplate is kept strictly on your local machine under your user profile directory.
Configurations are stored in config.yaml under the rTmp folder in your AppData directory.
Logs are written to log.txt under the rTmp folder in your AppData directory.
rTemplate does not upload your local configuration files or logs to any remote server.
